UI/UX Description

This webpage represents a high-end bar and restaurant landing page focused on a sophisticated, immersive dining experience. The layout is designed with a dark, modern aesthetic, highlighted by gold accents and neon-style lighting to create a luxurious ambiance. The main screen is dominated by a wide photo of the restaurant's interior, featuring moody, atmospheric lighting and stylish furniture, immediately giving visitors a sense of the environment. On the right, there are three key blocks with relevant imagery:

The Menu shows gourmet dishes on modern plating.

The Reservation section uses a lifestyle shot of a customer enjoying a fine dining atmosphere.

The Our Restaurant area features a wide-angle shot of the bar area for transparency and invitation.
A fixed header includes navigation links for menu, about, and reservations. CTAs are clear, accessible, and visually distinct. Social media icons are subtly placed in the corner for modern branding.
